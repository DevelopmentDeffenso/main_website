[LEGAL] Member Agreement — Deffenso Hackers Academy
01

Acceptance of Terms

By joining Deffenso Hackers Academy & Development ("the Academy"), all members unconditionally agree to strictly follow ethical, legal, and professional standards at all times. These terms constitute a binding agreement between the member and the Academy.

The Academy reserves the right to amend these terms at any time. Continued participation following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Binding Nature

By joining or continuing participation in the Academy, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms outlined in this document. Non-compliance may result in immediate termination and legal action.

02

Ethical Conduct &
Prohibited Activities

Critical

The Academy is committed to promoting ethical hacking practices within legal boundaries. The following activities are strictly prohibited:

Unauthorized access to any computer system, network, or digital property
Data theft, exfiltration, or unauthorized data access of any kind
Misuse of cybersecurity tools, exploits, or techniques against non-consenting parties
Any activity that constitutes illegal hacking under applicable laws
Distribution of malware, ransomware, or harmful software
Phishing, social engineering, or fraud targeting real individuals or organizations without explicit consent
Enforcement

Violations will result in immediate termination of membership and may be reported to relevant law enforcement authorities.

03

Confidentiality &
Information Security

Members are bound by a duty of confidentiality with respect to all Academy resources, tools, knowledge, and internal communications.

Protect all sensitive information obtained through Academy participation
Do not share internal tools, methodologies, or course materials with unauthorized individuals
Do not disclose member details, internal discussions, or operational strategies externally
Exercise caution when discussing Academy activities on public platforms or social media
04

Member Responsibilities
& Participation

All members are expected to uphold the highest standards of professionalism and active engagement:

Actively participate in training sessions and assigned programs
Complete tasks and assessments within designated timeframes
Maintain discipline and a growth-oriented mindset throughout participation
Contribute constructively to the Academy community and peer learning
Report any suspected security violations or unethical behavior to Academy leadership
05

Code of Conduct &
Community Standards

Zero Tolerance

Deffenso is a professional, merit-based community. Members must treat all individuals with respect and dignity. The following behaviors are absolutely prohibited:

Harassment, bullying, or targeted intimidation of any member
Discrimination based on race, gender, nationality, religion, or any protected characteristic
Toxic, abusive, or demeaning behavior in any Academy channel or activity
Spreading misinformation or deliberately harmful content within the community

Members are expected to:

Engage in respectful, constructive communication at all times
Support fellow members and contribute to a positive learning environment
06

Monitoring, Enforcement
& Policy Updates

The Academy reserves the right to monitor member activities within Academy platforms to ensure compliance with these terms and to maintain security and quality standards.

Performance evaluations may be conducted periodically. The Academy may suspend or terminate membership at its sole discretion for any violation of these terms or conduct deemed detrimental to the Academy's mission.

Policies may be updated at any time. Members will be notified of significant changes through official Academy channels.

07

Lawful Use & Liability

Members acknowledge their sole and complete responsibility to use all skills, tools, and knowledge acquired through the Academy exclusively for lawful, authorized, and ethical purposes.

The Academy is not liable for any misuse of knowledge or tools by its members. Any member who engages in illegal activity using skills obtained through the Academy does so entirely at their own risk and legal responsibility.

By continuing participation, members affirm their commitment to contribute positively to the global cybersecurity and development community.

Legal Responsibility

Misuse of Academy knowledge or tools constitutes a violation of this agreement and applicable law. The Academy will cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities in any investigation involving member misconduct.
