[PRIVACY] Data Protection Statement — Deffenso Hackers Academy
01

Introduction

Deffenso Hackers Academy & Development ("the Academy", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all member information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data in connection with Academy membership and activities.

As a cybersecurity-focused organization, we hold ourselves to the highest data protection standards — the same standards we teach our members to uphold.

02

Information We Collect

Transparency

We collect information necessary for Academy operations and member management:

Data Category Examples Purpose
Identity Information Name, username, profile details Member identification & access control
Contact Information Email address, messaging handle Communication & notifications
Activity Data Training logs, task completions, participation records Performance evaluation & progress tracking
Platform Usage Login times, accessed resources, tool usage Security monitoring & quality assurance
Communications Messages sent within Academy channels Safety, conduct compliance & record keeping
03

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used exclusively for lawful Academy purposes:

Managing and administering your Academy membership
Facilitating training programs and evaluating member progress
Monitoring compliance with Academy terms, conduct standards, and applicable laws
Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access or misuse
Communicating important Academy updates, policy changes, or security alerts
Improving Academy programs, tools, and resources
We do NOT sell or commercially exploit your personal data
We do NOT share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
04

Monitoring &
Activity Tracking

Important Notice

In accordance with our Terms & Conditions, the Academy reserves the right to monitor member activities conducted within Academy platforms, channels, and systems. This monitoring serves to:

Detect and prevent violations of Academy terms or ethical standards
Ensure the security and integrity of Academy infrastructure
Maintain quality control over training programs and outcomes
Investigate suspected misconduct or unauthorized activity
No Expectation of Privacy

Members should have no expectation of privacy when using Academy-owned platforms, tools, or communication channels for Academy activities.

05

Data Sharing
& Disclosure

We do not disclose member information to external parties except in the following circumstances:

When required by applicable law or a lawful order from a competent authority
When necessary to investigate, prevent, or respond to suspected illegal activity conducted by a member using Academy resources or knowledge
To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Academy, its members, or the public
With trusted service providers who support Academy operations under strict confidentiality obligations
Law Enforcement

Any disclosure to law enforcement authorities will be made in full cooperation where a member's activities are suspected to violate applicable cybercrime laws.

06

Data Security

As a cybersecurity-focused organization, we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect member data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
Encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit
Regular security audits of Academy systems and infrastructure
Incident response procedures for suspected data breaches
Member Responsibility

Members are also responsible for maintaining the security of their own credentials and must promptly report any suspected unauthorized access to Academy leadership.

07

Member Data Rights

Your Rights

Members may exercise the following rights with respect to their personal data held by the Academy:

Access
Request access to the personal information we hold about you
Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
Deletion
Request deletion of your data upon termination of membership, subject to legal retention obligations
Objection
Object to specific uses of your data where permitted by applicable law
How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact Academy leadership through official channels. Requests will be reviewed and responded to in a timely manner.

08

Data Retention
& Policy Updates

Member data is retained for as long as membership is active and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, or legitimate operational purposes. Activity and conduct records may be retained indefinitely where relevant to a security incident or legal matter.

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable regulations. Members will be notified of material changes through official Academy channels. Continued participation following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
